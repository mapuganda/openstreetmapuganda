Membership

Become a MapUganda Member

Thanks for your interest in joining and supporting MapUganda. Members of the organisation are entitled to vote in the affairs of the organisatioin. Anyone interest in influencing the activities of the organization is welcome to join. Please note that, you do not have to be a member in order to use or contribute to OpenStreetMap.

MapUganda is an independent non-governmental, not for profit organisation focused on growing the OpenStreetMap and Open Data community in Uganda and promotes community mapping, generates map awareness, actively pledges open data sets and builds a network of enthusiastic mappers in Uganda. All activities are set up around the global OpenStreetMap (OSM) project. OSM is a map and database whereby the stored geographic data is owned by the contributors themselves, allowing everyone to view, create, use this information for free, in new and innovative ways.

Membership Fee: UGX 50,000 per year!
