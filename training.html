Training Services

GIS and Mapping Training

Did you know that for whatever you do, you need data? At Map Uganda we map for resilience, providing solutions to problems using maps and geospatial data. We offer and GIS training suitable orgaizations, companies and individuals to equip them with the necessary tools they use in their fields of expertise. We offer customized GIS training for your current employment/field of study.
We aso offer training in different packages basic, intermediate and advanced GIS, field data collection using mobile applications and creating both static and dynamic/interactive maps.

Our training services include;

  • Data collection
  • Data Analysis
  • GIS training/Analysis using GIS
  • Making Maps/Cartography
  • Imagery processing
  • Online interactive maps

Do you want to get involved?

We have a team of experienced trainers in GIS ready to equip you with the skills needed to become a proffessional GIS personnel.
